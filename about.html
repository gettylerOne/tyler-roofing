How it started

From an apprentice's roof to a small business.

Jacob Tyler worked for three years alongside a brother-in-law at an established roofing company — long enough to learn the trade properly, long enough to know how he wanted to run his own outfit.

In 2023, his brother-in-law told him it was time. Time to start his own company and time to start his own family. He took both pieces of advice. Tyler Roofing & Home Solutions opened that year, headquartered in Columbiana and serving the Birmingham metro.

Today the company runs two to three crews across Shelby and Jefferson counties — 25 years of combined roofing experience between them. Over 100 jobs since opening. Same standard he learned at the start.
