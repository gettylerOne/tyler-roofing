01

What we collect

When you book an inspection, request a quote, or contact us, we collect what you give us:

  • Name, phone number, email
  • Property address — needed to inspect the roof
  • Notes you provide about the project, damage, or insurance situation
  • Preferred contact times and method

We also collect basic site analytics — page views, referring URLs, device type — through standard tools like Google Analytics. We use this to understand what's useful on the site, not to identify you.

02

Why we collect it

So we can do the job you asked us to do — schedule an inspection, give you a quote, coordinate with your insurance carrier, and follow up.

If you don't want us to contact you anymore, tell us and we'll stop. Reply STOP to any text we send, or email tylerroofingandhomesolutions@gmail.com.

03

Who we share it with

We share your information with people you ask us to involve in your project:

  • Insurance carriers and adjusters — when you've filed a claim and want us to coordinate
  • Financing partners — when you ask us to connect you for financing
  • Subcontractors — only as needed to schedule the work (gutter, painting subs, etc.)
  • Manufacturers — for warranty registration on your behalf

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with advertisers or third-party marketers. Period.

04

Cookies & tracking

This site uses standard browser cookies for analytics (to count visitors and understand which pages are useful) and to remember your preferences.

We don't run remarketing pixels, behavioral ad networks, or third-party tracking that follows you across the web.

05

Text messaging (SMS)

When you give us your phone number, we may text you to confirm appointments, update on scheduling, or follow up on your quote. Message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for help. We don't sell phone numbers and we don't share them outside the people working on your job.

06

Your rights

You can:

  • Ask us what we have on file about you
  • Ask us to correct anything that's wrong
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Tell us to stop contacting you

Email tylerroofingandhomesolutions@gmail.com or call 205-547-0122. We'll handle it within a reasonable time — usually a few business days.

07

How long we keep it

Project records (contract, scope, photos, warranty paperwork) we keep for the life of the warranty plus three years, in case you have a warranty question.

Lead inquiries that don't turn into projects we keep for one year, then delete.

Site analytics roll up to anonymized aggregate after 14 months.
