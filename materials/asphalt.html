✓ What's good

The pros.

  • 30-year typical warranty (some lifetime-limited)
  • Strong wind ratings — 110-130 mph stock, more with enhanced nailing
  • Wide range of colors and looks
  • Most common material — easy to source, fair pricing
! Honest trade-offs

The cons.

  • Shorter lifespan than metal or slate
  • Granule loss over time — especially in hail-prone areas
  • Heat absorption higher than light-colored metal

How asphalt
goes on a Birmingham home.

Step-by-step. Most jobs run 1-3 days on the roof depending on size, pitch, and weather.

01
Tear off existing shingle and underlayment to the deck
02
Inspect decking — replace any soft or rotted spans
03
Install ice and water shield at valleys and penetrations
04
Synthetic underlayment, 4-nail or 6-nail pattern per spec
05
Architectural shingle in the chosen profile and color
06
Ridge vent, hip and ridge cap, pipe boots, step flashing
07
