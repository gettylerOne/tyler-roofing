✓ What's good

The pros.

  • 40-50+ year lifespan typical
  • Class A fire rating, Class 4 impact (most profiles)
  • Reflective — measurable energy savings in AL summers
  • Wind ratings up to 140 mph
  • Looks distinctive — modern or classic farmhouse
! Honest trade-offs

The cons.

  • Higher upfront cost than shingle
  • Requires experienced install — fewer crews qualified
  • Noisier in heavy rain (mitigated by proper underlayment)
  • Can show oil-canning on long flat panels

How metal

02

03
Drip edge, valley pans, custom flashing fabricated for the job
04
Standing-seam panels run to length — no horizontal seams when avoidable
05
Mechanical or snap-lock seam, concealed clips
