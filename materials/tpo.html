✓ What's good

The pros.

  • Only material that works on flat roofs
  • TPO reflects heat — energy savings
  • 20-30 year typical lifespan with proper install
  • Repairable in place — no full tear-off for most leaks
! Honest trade-offs

The cons.

  • Not for high-slope — a different system entirely
  • Punctures from foot traffic are the main failure mode
  • Requires specialist install (heat-weld for TPO)
