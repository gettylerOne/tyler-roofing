✓ What's good

The pros.

  • Look of slate or shake at ~1/2-1/3 the weight
  • Class A fire rating, Class 4 impact (most lines)
  • 50+ year warranty typical
  • No maintenance — won't crack like real slate, won't rot like wood
  • Wider color palette than natural materials
! Honest trade-offs

The cons.

  • Higher cost than architectural shingle
  • Still niche — fewer installers, longer lead times
  • Some HOAs in historic districts won't accept synthetics
