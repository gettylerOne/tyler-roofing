Four questions, every time. 1) Are you licensed in Alabama? — required by the State Licensing Board on residential jobs over $10,000. 2) Will you handle the insurance claim if there's storm damage? 3) What warranties do you offer? — you want both a workmanship warranty (covering install errors) AND a manufacturer warranty (covering material defects), in writing. 4) How do you handle cleanup and landscaping? — end-of-day magnet sweep for nails, tarps under work areas to protect plants and lawn.
