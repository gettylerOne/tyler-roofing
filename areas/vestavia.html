What we see on Vestavia Hills roofs

Local notes.

Homes here often need decking inspection on tear-off — older OSB and the occasional plank deck. We pull-and-replace on bad spans. Cahaba Heights and Liberty Park lean newer.

Storm corridor

After the weather.

Vestavia gets the same Shelby County storm cells. The ridge orientation channels wind down the valleys — wind-only roof loss is more common here than hail in some events.
