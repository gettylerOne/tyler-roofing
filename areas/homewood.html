What we see on Homewood roofs

Local notes.

Edgewood and Hollywood bungalows often have intricate roof lines for the size — multiple gables, dormers, low-pitch porch tie-ins. We've gotten quick at these. Tight access is the main challenge — we coordinate carefully with neighbors.
