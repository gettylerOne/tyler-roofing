What we see on Columbiana roofs

Local notes.

Mix of older downtown homes near the courthouse, mid-century ranches on the surrounding streets, and newer subdivisions out toward 25 and Highway 70. We do a lot of repair work on the older stock and full replacements on the newer subdivisions as they age out of their original 25-year shingles.

Storm corridor

After the weather.

The Shelby County hail corridor runs through Columbiana every spring. The 2023 events brought weeks of claim work to the area. We respond same-day for active leaks and tarp-ups.
